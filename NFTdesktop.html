Description

The Orbitian
is a collection of 10,000 unique NFTs on the
Ethereum
blockchain,

There are all sorts of beings in the NFT
Universe. The
most advanced and friendly of
the bunch are Orbitians.

They live in a metal space machines, high up
in the sky
and only have one foot on Earth.

These Orbitians are a peaceful race, but they
have been
war with a group of invaders for
many generations.
The invaders are called
Upside-Downs, because of their
inverted
bodies that live on the ground, yet do not
know any other way to be. Upside-Downs
believe that
will be able to win this war
if they could only get
an eye into
Orbitian territory, so they've taken to make
human beings their target.
